This is the Ping-Pong Test

This simple application will take an input from you and create an array from 1 to your number. Then take all the numbers divisible by 15 and replace them with the word "ping-pong", all the numbers divisible by 5 and replace them with "pong", and all numbers divisible by 3 and replace them with "ping"

Click here and enter a number that is greater than zero.
